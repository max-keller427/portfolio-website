About Me

Current Job: Front Desk/Developer at The Front Climbing Club

Originally from Dallas, TX, I developed a passion for being outside through skateboarding. My longing for adventure lead me to the coast of Texas, where I pursued (and received) a bachelors degree in marine biology. In the midst of my last semester of college, I discovered the world of rock climbing which promptly lead me to the second driest state in the nation, Utah.

A conversation with a dear friend from home sparked my interest in development about a year ago. I wanted a way to see the world, support my friends and family and have an engaging and stimulating career and development seemed like a great choice. I started my career as a web developer with the help of Lambda School.
